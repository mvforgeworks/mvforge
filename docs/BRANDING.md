## 3. Product Deep Dives

### 3.1 AuditForge — Compliance & Security Forge

**Audience:** MSPs, DevOps teams, internal audit, veteran IT consultants, gov contractors.

**Promise:** Transform complex compliance, security, and infrastructure audits (network configs, IaC, cloud/SRE) into automated, traceable, professional deliverables — faster, cheaper, sovereign.

**Differentiators:**
- AI analysis (Grok/xAI + Ollama local) of configs, Terraform/Ansible, logs
- One-click PDF/DOCX with evidence chaining
- Self-hosted via CasaOS/Forgejo + MCP integrations
- Veteran-focused templates (VA claims audit parallels, DoD-aligned patterns)

**GTM:** Content-led (audit tutorials, LinkedIn veteran engineer stories) → free self-hosted tier → paid cloud/SaaS or enterprise support. VR&E and veteran accelerator partnerships.

**Pricing (target):** Freemium + $49–299/mo + engagement/success fees.

**Unit economics:** Target 60%+ gross margin. Bootstrap on homelab. Cross-sell Sovereign Dashboard.

---

### 3.2 DomainForge — Domain Intelligence & Lead-Gen Forge

**Audience:** Indie hackers, agencies, Web3 builders, network engineers needing vanity/custom domains.

**Promise:** Scan, value, auction, and monetize expiring/undervalued domains with automated lead-gen — privacy-first, self-hosted crawler.

**Differentiators:**
- Real-time expiring domain scanner + AI valuation
- Automated outreach tied to Forge Network / Resume Wars funnel
- Portfolio dashboard synced to Sovereign Dashboard
- Mullvad/OPNsense privacy integration

**GTM:** Freemium scanner → premium alerts/auctions ($29–99/mo) → done-for-you flipping (10–20% commission). “Domain Wars” content series.

**Unit economics:** Target 80%+ margin. Low variable cost, data moat from networking expertise.

---

### 3.3 ForgeWorks — Professional Services & Workshop Forge

**Audience:** Veterans, remote MSPs, self-hosting enthusiasts, engineers claiming infrastructure sovereignty.

**Promise:** Fixed-price packages and workshops that bridge open-source tools and production reality.

**Offer examples:**
- Sovereign Homelab in a Box (OPNsense, WireGuard, CasaOS)
- CCNA mastery workshops (PacketMan)
- White-glove consulting tied to AuditForge/DomainForge outputs
- Veteran-priority pricing · VR&E-aligned pathways

**GTM:** Free content/webinars → workshops → engagements → SaaS upsell. LinkedIn, veteran groups, private community (HumHub-style).

**Pricing (target):** $2k–15k projects + retainers.

**Unit economics:** Immediate cash flow while SaaS matures. Target 50%+ margin scaling to product-led.

---

### 3.4 Sovereign Dashboard — Command Center

**Role:** One pane of glass for monitoring, deploying, and scaling all Forge projects.

**Strategic value:** Cross-sell hub, integration moat, visible proof of “sovereign tech” narrative.

**Monetization:** Paid tier atop freemium tool funnel (SRE Forge, PacketMan, etc.).

## 5. Business & Financial Architecture

**Monetization mix:**
- Freemium tools → paid Sovereign Dashboard
- Forge Claims success fees
- Productized deploys (VPN, Ansible)
- Domain auction lead-gen
- VR&E / SBA non-dilutive capital

**Operating model:** Remote-first, homelab-backed (CasaOS, Forgejo, Nextcloud), low overhead, disability-aligned.

**North star:** Compounding via veteran + dev communities; path to 100% VA + business revenue = full sovereignty.

**Risk posture:** Track unit economics ruthlessly; high gross margins on software; services fund product development.
